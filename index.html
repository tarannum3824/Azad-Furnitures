About Us

Located in Narsinghpur, Azad Furniture has been serving families with premium handcrafted wooden furniture since 2011. Owned by MR. Shekh Azad Khan, we specialize in manufacturing and trading quality wooden furniture including center tables, chairs, beds, and custom designs that turn houses into homes.
